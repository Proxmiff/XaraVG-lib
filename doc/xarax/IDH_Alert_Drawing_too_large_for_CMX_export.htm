Message: Drawing is too large for CMX export

Cause

The drawing you are exporting is more than 40 inches across. You can only save drawings in CMX 5 format if they are smaller than 40 inches across.

What Can I Do?

 You do not need to do anything. Xara LX has automatically scaled your drawing so it fits.

 If you do not want the drawing scaled, use another format like CMX 6 which does not have a limit on page size.
