Selector Tool - Rotation Field

The Rotation field on the Selector Tool Infobar lets you rotate the selection. To rotate the selection:

 Type a value in degrees into the field and press RETURN.

 Click the arrow buttons.

Positive angles rotate the selection anti clockwise, negative angles clockwise. The rotation is centered around the transformation center. This is usually be in the center of the selection unless it has been moved.
