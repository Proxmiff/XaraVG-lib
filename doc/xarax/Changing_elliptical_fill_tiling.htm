Changing the tiling of elliptical fills

You can change elliptical fills so the outer color extends out to infinity or so the fill repeats as a series of rings.

 To change the tiling

1. Select the object (see Selecting objects).

2. Choose the Fill Tool.

3. Select Simple or Repeating from the menu on the Fill Tool Infobar.
