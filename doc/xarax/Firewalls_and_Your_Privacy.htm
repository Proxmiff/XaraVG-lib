Firewalls and your Privacy

 

If you own a software firewall, it will likely warn you that Xara LX is trying to contact Xara's servers. There is no need for concern, and you can authorize LX's communications.

Xara LX contacts the Xara.com servers in order to fetch latest update information about the program version and demo movies you can download. It does NOT send any personal information to Xara.

Xara takes your privacy very seriously, and you can find out more in our corporate privacy statement.
