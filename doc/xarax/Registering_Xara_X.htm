Registering Xara LX

Registering your copy of Xara LX is important so that you are eligible for discounts on future upgrades and other Xara Products.

If you bought direct from Xara, please sign in with the Xara Online username and password that you used to purchase your copy of Xara LX. If you bought your copy of Menu Maker from somewhere other than Xara, and don't have a Xara Online account, please click the sign-up button to create yourself an account.

You will be prompted to register Xara LX after you have install the program. If you told Xara LX to remind you later, but have decided to register before you are reminded again, you can select Register from the Help menu in Xara LX.
