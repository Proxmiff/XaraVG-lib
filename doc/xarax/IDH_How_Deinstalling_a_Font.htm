De-installing fonts

The Font Gallery provides you with an easy way of de-installing (removing) fonts from your computer.

 To De-install fonts

1. From the Utilities menu, choose Font Gallery.

2. Find and select the required font in the Installed Fonts section of the gallery (see Selecting items in a gallery).

3. Click the De-install button in the gallery.

Notes

 You can only de-install fonts from the Installed Fonts section of the Gallery.

 Some programs continue to show a de-installed font in their font list until you quit and then reload them.
