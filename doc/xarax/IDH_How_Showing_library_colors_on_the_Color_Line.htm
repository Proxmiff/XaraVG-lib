Showing library colors on the Color Line

You can display any of the palettes listed in the Color Gallery on the Color Line or turn off any you do not want to see on the Color Line.

 To turn on/off a palette

1. From the Utilities menu, choose Color Gallery.

2. Right-click on the library of colors to open the pop-up menu.

3. Select or deselect Show In Color Line.
