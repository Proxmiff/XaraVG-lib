Xara Webster was a drawing package from Xara and now replaced by Xara LX.

Xara Webster owners will find much of Xara LX familiar. The main difference is the extra tools and options in Xara LX.

You can load and import all Xara Webster files into Xara LX.

Xara Webster can load WEB and XAR files but some Xara LX features do not exist in Xara Webster. Your drawings may look slightly different when viewed in Xara Webster.
