Drawing ellipses and circles

You can draw ellipses and circles using either the Ellipse Tool or the QuickShape Tool. (The Ellipse Tool is generally more convenient to use.)

 To draw an ellipse or circle

1. Choose the Ellipse Tool or QuickShape Tool. (If you use the QuickShape Tool, click the Ellipse button on the QuickShape Tool Infobar)

2. To draw an ellipse select the Bounds Creation Button on the Infobar. Drag diagonally to create the ellipse.

3. To draw a circle select either

 Click the Radius Creation Button on the Infobar. Create the circle by dragging from the center outwards.

 Click the Diameter Creation Button on the Infobar. Create the circle by dragging across the diameter of the circle.

Tips

 Holding down CTRL during a By Bounds drag creates a circle instead of an ellipse.

 Holding down Shift during the drag forces creation from the center of the ellipse or circle.
