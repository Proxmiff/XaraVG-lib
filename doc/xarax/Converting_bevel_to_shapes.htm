Converting bevels to shapes

You can convert a beveled object into a group containing the bevel and the object. The bevel is converted to a shape and, after ungrouping, can be edited. Note that after converting a bevel to a shape, you cannot use the Bevel Tool to edit it as it is no longer a bevel.

 To convert a beveled object to shapes

1. Select the bevel or the object (see Selecting objects).

2. From the Arrange menu, choose Convert to Editable Shapes.

Tip

 To convert a bevel quickly, right-click on the bevel or the object. This opens the pop-up menu. Choose Convert To Editable Shapes.
