Adding curved corners to polygons

Polygons created using the QuickShape Tool can have either sharp or rounded corners. After choosing rounded corners, you can change the radius of the corners to increase or decrease the curvature. For more details on changing the curved corners, see Changing curved corners on a polygon.

 To add curved corners to a polygon

1. Select the polygon (see Selecting objects).

2. Choose the QuickShape Tool.

3. Click the Curved Corners button on the QuickShape Tool Infobar.

To remove curved corners, click Curved Corners again.

Tips

 You can also apply curved corners to star-shaped polygons.

 You can also make the corners rounded by double-clicking on the corner.
