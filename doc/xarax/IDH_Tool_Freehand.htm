Freehand & Brush Tool F3

The Freehand & Brush Tool allows you to draw freehand lines and shapes as you would with a pen on paper. You can edit freehand lines in the same way as those drawn with the Shape Editor Tool or Pen Tool. It can also be used to extend and redraw sections of existing lines and shapes. To find out more about lines and shapes, see the Lines and Shapes Overview
