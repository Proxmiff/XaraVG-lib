Deleting points on lines and shapes

Once you have drawn a line or shape, you can remove individual point handles from it.

 To delete points on lines and shapes

1. Select the line or shape (see Selecting objects).

2. Choose the Shape Editor Tool.

3. Select the point handles.

4. Click the Delete Point(s) Button on the Shape Editor Tool Infobar.

Tip

 To quickly delete points, right click on one of the selected points to open the pop-up menu and choose Delete Points.
