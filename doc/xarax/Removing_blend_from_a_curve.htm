Removing blend from a curve

You can easily remove a blend from a curved line or shape.

 To remove text from a curve

1. Select the blend (see Selecting objects).

2. Select the Blend Tool.

3. Deselect the Blend along a curve button.

Tip

 You can still edit the blend when it is on a curve. You can also reshape the curve without removing the blend.
