Modifying a bevel

Bevels give objects an appearance of depth instead of being flat on the page. The beveling effect is created by a light shining across a shaped, bevel edge. You can change the angle and strength of the light and change the shape (the profile or "Type") and size of the bevel edge.

What do you want to change?

 the bevel shape (the bevel Type)

 the size of the bevel

 the direction of the light source

 the contrast across the bevel

 the bevel color or fill effect

 the bevel join style

 whether the bevel is inside or outside the object
