Searching a gallery

Several Galleries contain many items. You can easily search for the items you want.

 To search a gallery

1. Click the Options button in the gallery.

2. Choose Find from the menu to open the Gallery Find dialog box.

3. Type in the text to search for.

4. Choose whether you wish to search just the names and keywords.

5. Click the Find First button to find the first match.

Click the Find Next button to continue searching.

Tip

 For more information on the options, click the Help button in the dialog box.
