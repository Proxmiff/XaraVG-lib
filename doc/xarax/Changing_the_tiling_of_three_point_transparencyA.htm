Changing the tiling of three point transparency

You can change three point transparency and four point transparency so the transparency extends out to infinity or so the transparency pattern repeats.

 To change the tiling

1. Select the object (see Selecting objects).

2. Choose the Transparency Tool.

3. Select Simple or Repeating from the menu on the Transparency Tool Infobar.
