Gallery Docking and Undocking

It is possible to dock one or more galleries on either side of the LX window

 To dock a gallery

1. Open a gallery by clicking an icon on the gallery control bar or selecting Utilities then Galleries.

2. Drag the gallery to either the left or right hand side of your screen.

3. Repeat the process for other galleries as required.

 To undock a gallery

Clicking an icon on the gallery control bar will remove the gallery from screen. But if you click it again, the gallery will return in its docked position. To undock the gallery completely, just drag the gallery away from the side of the screen and it will undock.

 Reorganizing galleries

If more than one gallery is docked on one side, they will share a column together. You can resize the galleries as required, and drag the galleries up or down to change their order accordingly.

 Resetting the galleries

If you wish to reset all control bars and galleries to their default state, select Window, then Control Bars then click Reset.
