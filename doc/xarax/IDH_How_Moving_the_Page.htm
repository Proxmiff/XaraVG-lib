Moving the page

Often you want to scroll the document around in the window so you can see different parts of it.

 To move the page

1. Choose the Push Tool.

2. Move the pointer over the page and drag to slide the page around in the window.

Tip

 You can also move the page using the scrollbars. See Using scrollbars.

 If your mouse has a wheel, you can:

 scroll the wheel to scroll up and down

 or hold down Shift to scroll left (scroll the wheel up) or left (wheel down)

 or hold the wheel down to slide the document around in the window.
