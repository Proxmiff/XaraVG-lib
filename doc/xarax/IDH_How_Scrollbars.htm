Using scrollbars

Scrollbars allow you to move the page around in the Xara LX document window. The Scrollbars in Xara LX are proportional. For example if the current zoom on your document means you are looking at half the workspace, the scroll bar will take up half of the bar. This makes it easier to see, at a glance, at what scale the document is being viewed. The scrollbars are also interactive meaning that the page scrolls as you move them, rather than waiting until you release the mouse button.

Tips

 Clicking the right mouse button on scrollbars moves in the opposite direction from normal. For example, right-clicking above the scroll box scrolls down. This makes it simple to move backwards and forwards using the scrollbars without having to move the mouse.

 You can change the size of the scrollbars. See Customizing the appearance of the program.
