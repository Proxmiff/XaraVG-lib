View Pop-up Menu

If the pop-up menu is opened with the mouse pointer over a blank area of the document or an unselected handle, it contains the following:

 New View

 Full Screen

 - - - - - - - - - -

 Quality

 Show Grid

 Show Guides

 - - - - - - - - - -

 Snap to Grid

 Snap to Guides

 Snap to Objects

 - - - - - - - - - -

 Default Page Background

 Page Options
