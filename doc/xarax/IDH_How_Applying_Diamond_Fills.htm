Applying diamond fills

You can fill objects with any of LX's different fill types including diamond fills.

 To apply a diamond fill

1. Select the object (see Selecting objects).

2. Choose the Fill Tool.

3. Choose Diamond from the Fill Type drop-down list on the Fill Tool Infobar.

4. Drag from the center of the fill to create the diamond shape the way you want it. This creates a square diamond fill, drag the fill handles to stretch or squash the fill.

5. If you want a repeating fill pattern, select Repeating from the Fill Tiling drop-down list.

Tip

 You can later edit the position, extent and angle of the fill by dragging the fill handles. See Changing diamond fills for details.
