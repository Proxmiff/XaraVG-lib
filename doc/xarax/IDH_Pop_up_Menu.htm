Bitmap Pop-up Menu

If the pop-up menu is opened with the mouse pointer over a bitmap, it contains the following options:

 

 Special Effects

 Bitmap Tracer

 - - - - - - - - - -

 Cut

 Copy

 Paste

 - - - - - - - - - -

 Delete

 Duplicate

 Clone

 - - - - - - - - - -

 Convert to Editable Shapes

 Create Bitmap Copy

 Combine Shapes

 - - - - - - - - - -

 Imagesetting

 - - - - - - - - - -

 Web Address

 Color Editor
