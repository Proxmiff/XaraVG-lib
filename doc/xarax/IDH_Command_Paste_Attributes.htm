Paste Attributes (Edit Menu) CTRL+Shift+A

This option lets you copy attributes (such as the line pattern or fill color) between objects. It is a quick way of applying multiple attributes. When you choose Paste Attributes, the attributes of the objects on the clipboard are applied to all the objects currently selected. The objects remain on the clipboard until overwritten by, for example, a Cut or Copy operation so you can repeat Paste Attributes many times.
