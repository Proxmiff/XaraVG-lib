Drawing lines

You can use the Pen, Shape Editor or Freehand & Brush Tools to draw lines.

 The Shape Editor Tool is the most flexible and can also edit lines.

 The Pen Tool is provided for users familiar with programs such as CorelDRAW and Adobe Illustrator.

 The Freehand & Brush Tool lets you draw and edit lines by following freehand mouse movements. You can also use the Freehand & Brush Tool to apply brush strokes and stroke shapes to lines.
