Changing the level in flat transparency

Flat transparency is the simplest type of transparency. The only control is over the transparency level.

 To change the level in a flat transparency

1. Select the object (see Selecting objects).

2. Choose the Transparency Tool.

3. Move the Transparency Slider on the Transparency Tool Infobar. Or for precise control, type into the text box to the right.
